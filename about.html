About Us

Welcome to Janakalyan Blood Bank

The start..

The Janakalyan Blood Bank Nashik was started in August 1989 with the objective of providing safe blood to the patients in Nashik city and its vicinity.The Blood Bank initially started from a small place at Canada Corner, Sharanpur Road, Nasik .Now Organization is at Old Gangapur Naka, Gangapur Road, Nasik.

It has got a state of the art infrastructure including the latest machines for testing of blood samples for disorders, before the blood is sent for distribution.

The Blood Bank operates under the strict norms prescribed by the Central Government without any kind of deviation adhering to the extremely stringent licensing policies of the Food and Drug Administration (FDA).

Our Area.


About 14000 sq.ft.It is the only blood bank with an area of one foot and all areas are used for blood bank only and various dimensions of blood donation movement e.g. The blood bank also has separate venues for blood donation awareness, training, thalassemia center, blood donation camp organizers.

100% safe blood

All types of state-of-the-art systems, equipment, laboratories are available in the blood bank and 100% safe blood is provided by performing the necessary tests.50 workers are working 24 hours for all these works.This includes doctors, trained technicians, staff and staff, all of whom receive regular specialized training in their respective fields.From it the knowledge of his work and subject is kept up to date.100% safe blood transfusion.

Recognized by NACO

Janakalyan Blood Bank is recognized by NACO (National Aids Control Organization) and is the first blood bank in North Maharashtra to get the status of Divisional Blood Collection Center from State Blood Transfusion Council.All the rules made by the Government of India and Maharashtra State Food and Drug Administration are strictly followed and no compromise is made.Therefore, safe blood is known as Janakalyan Blood Bank.

Service organization

RSS Janakalyan Samiti is a service organization.The Board of Directors of the Blood Bank consists of eminent persons from various fields (Entrepreneurs, Doctors, Independent Professionals, CAs) in Nashik city and district.Facilities of single donor platelets prepared.The blood bank is a charitable organisation managed by devoted trustees from different walks of life including professionals and industrialists, who work on honorary basis.

Patient services

The Jankalyan Blood Bank run by it carries out patient services in which:

  1. Concession in service charges to needy patients.
  2. Blood transfusions available to patients in government hospitals and charitable hospitals at a discount.
  3. Free blood and blood transfusions for children with balasemia.
  4. The amount of free medical treatment and check up for thalassemia patients is more than Rs. 13 lakhs per annum.
